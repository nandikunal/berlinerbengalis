About Berliner Bengalis e.V.

Our Mission & Values

Berliner Bengalis e.V. (Berlin's Bengali Association) is a registered non-profit community dedicated to preserving and promoting authentic Bengali culture, heritage, and Hindu Sanatani traditions in Berlin.

Our Vision & Impact

We envision Berlin as a place where Bengali traditions flourish, Sanatani values thrive, and our community feels at home away from home.

Through Durga Puja celebrations and cultural activities, we honor our heritage while promoting environmental consciousness and community responsibility.

What We Do

  • Durga Puja Celebrations: Annual festival featuring grand pandal, authentic rituals, and traditional cultural programs
  • Bengali Cultural Events: Music, dance, literature workshops, and artistic expressions
  • Community Building: Creating inclusive spaces where Bengalis connect, support each other, and celebrate together
  • Environmental Stewardship: Promoting eco-friendly and sustainable practices honoring Mother Earth
  • Youth Engagement: Programs dedicated to passing on traditions and cultural pride to the next generation
